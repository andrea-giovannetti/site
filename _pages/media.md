My research on online hate, extremism, and organized crime is meant to inform public debate and policy, so it regularly features in national media and in evidence to government inquiries. A selection is below.

### Coverage of the *Countering Violent Extremism (Phase 2)* study

As **principal investigator**, I led the study for the [Department of Home Affairs](https://www.homeaffairs.gov.au/) and the [Tackling Hate Lab](https://tacklinghate.org/) documenting the surge in online antisemitic and Islamophobic hate in Australia after 7 October 2023 (and again after the December 2025 Bondi attack). A central finding, that a small, incumbent core of accounts drives most of the hate and that these dynamics can be tracked to pre-empt escalation, was reported by two of Australia's major mastheads:

- [*Online hatred spiked after the Bondi massacre: Meta knows why* — The Sydney Morning Herald](https://www.smh.com.au/politics/nsw/online-hatred-spiked-after-the-bondi-massacre-meta-knows-why-20260612-p606d5.html)
- [*Meta to front royal commission to answer for the role media has played in amplifying hate* — The Age](https://www.theage.com.au/politics/nsw/online-hatred-spiked-after-the-bondi-massacre-meta-knows-why-20260612-p606d5.html)

You can read about the underlying project here: [Home Affairs — Countering Violent Extremism (Phase 2)]({{ '/projects/home_affairs_cve_phase2/' | relative_url }}).

### Tackling Hate Lab at the Royal Commission

As **Co-Director** of the [Tackling Hate Lab](https://tacklinghate.org/), I contribute to the Lab's broader evidence on the relationship between online hostility and real-world violence, which was presented to the Royal Commission on Antisemitism and Social Cohesion in 2026:

- [*Real-world incidents targeting Jews reinforce 'spiral of hate' online, royal commission hears* — ABC News](https://www.abc.net.au/news/2026-07-01/real-world-violence-strong-driver-of-online-anti-jewish-hate-nsw/106862394)
